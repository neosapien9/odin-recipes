Corn Soup

Description

One of the Caribbean's favorite after-party street foods. This savory soup packed with corn, spices and veggies will have you coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • Split peas
  • Corn
  • Sweet peppers
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Shadon beni
  • Ginger
  • Lemon Juice
  • Carrots

Steps

  1. boil 1 cup of split peas until soft
  2. Saute chopped onions and garlic in large pot
  3. pour split peas over saute and add extra water as needed
  4. add corn and any hard to boil veggies now [and dumplings]
  5. add 1 tbsp of olive oil and 1 tsp of salt
  6. After hard to boil ingredients are done, add sweet peppers, easy to boil veggies and seasoning and leave for another 5 mins
  7. when done let sit for 5 to 10 mins, then it's ready to be served
