Lentils

Description

This is probably one of my favourite go to staples. Paired with rice and a veggie side of your choice makes this meal complete

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Garlic
  • Cilantro or Shadon Beni
  • Ginger
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Salt

Preparation

  1. Sortey garlic in oil of choice
  2. Add 2 cups of water to pot
  3. Add 1 cup of lentils to pot
  4. Bring to boil
  5. Add salt as desired
  6. add 1 tbps of tomato Sauce
  7. add 1/2 tsp of Ginger
  8. when lentils are almost ready, add Cilantro
  9. simmer til water evaporates to desired
