Buckwheat Pancakes

Description

This quick and healthy take on a traditional breakfast has hints of nuttiness and goes well paired with butter and fresh berries

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup buckwheat flour
  • 1/2 Cup whole-wheat flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp Brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 cup of milk or milk substitute

Preparation

  1. Mix all dry ingredients in a big bowl
  2. Mix egg and half of the portion of milk in a smaller bowl
  3. Pour milk egg mixture into the dry mix and beat till batter consistency. add more milk as needed
  4. pre-Heat pan on medium heat
  5. pour mixture to desired size
  6. flip when bubbles appear on top
  7. Repeat step 5 and 6 for others. adjust heat as needed
